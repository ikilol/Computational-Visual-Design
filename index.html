TileTalks is a project developed in Computational Visual Design course at Politecnico di Milano for Dipartimento di Design. Reminiscent of the shapes of the masters of the milanese style and deeply influenced by Neue Bauhaus' composition rules, it exploits p5.js' potential to create dinamic and generative identities for 3 cycles of conferences.
